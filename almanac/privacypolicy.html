Personal Almanac Privacy policy

Below is the privacy policy for the Personal Almanac. We take your privacy seriously. We've had our personal data stolen and hacked just as you have.

The best way we can keep your data safe is to never send it to our servers. We don't want your data, we have enough of our own. The best way we can keep from having your data stolen from us is to never collect it in the first place.

Now, just like most mobile application we collect anonymous usage statistics. These are collected from your day-to-day interactions with this software in an effort to know where new features should be added and bugs should be squashed. Your continued use of this software is your consent for this anonymous usage data collection.

Your almanac entries are only yours. These are stored on your mobile device. If you have chosen to link a Google Calendar, your entries are still only yours. Once linked your entries will be sent to the Google Calendar you chose.
